One Stop Accounting Co., Ltd. offers comprehensive audit services performed by certified public accountants (CPAs) with extensive experience in both domestic and international standards. We serve a wide range of businesses including private limited companies, public companies, multinational corporations, and non-profit organizations.



Our Audit Services Include

  • Annual Financial Statement Audits in compliance with Thai Financial Reporting Standards (TFRS) and International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS)
  • Interim Financial Reviews to ensure accuracy and reliability of financial information
  • Statutory Audits in accordance with requirements from the Board of Investment (BOI), Department of Business Development, and the Stock Exchange of Thailand
  • Internal Audit Services to strengthen internal controls and enhance operational efficiency
  • Professional Accounting and Tax Advisory by experienced consultants
  • Due Diligence Audits and business valuation for mergers, acquisitions, and investments
  • International Audit Services tailored for businesses with overseas partners or operations
